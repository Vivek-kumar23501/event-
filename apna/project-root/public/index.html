About DAKSH

Know more about the legacy and vision of DAKSH

The Premier Tech Festival of Eastern India

DAKSH is the annual technical and cultural festival of DAKSH University, one of the premier technical and professional education institutes of India. The fest is a dazzling display of talent, innovation, and passion across a multitude of domains.

With a legacy of excellence spanning over a decade,DAKSH brings together the brightest minds from across the country to compete, collaborate, and celebrate the spirit of technology and culture.
